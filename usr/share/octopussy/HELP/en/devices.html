Devices

Here you can configure your Devices.
One Device is one source of logs. This source could send its logs through syslog or in an asynchronous way (FTP, SCP...).
Here you can:
  • modify Services list associated to this Device
  • modify Storages locations associated to this Device
  • edit information about this Device
  • show Dashboard about this Device
  • remove one Device
  • change parsing state: Start, Pause or Stop
